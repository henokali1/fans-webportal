Welcome To Fujairah Air Navigation Services

Fujairah Air Navigation Services (FANS) is an Air Navigation Services provider (ANSP) based in Fujairah, one of the seven emirates that make up UAE and the only one located completely on the eastern coast of the UAE. Fujairah Air Navigation Services was established under the directives and Saleh Bin Mohammed Al Sharqi Chairman of the Department of Industry and Economy and with the Department of Civil Aviation.
